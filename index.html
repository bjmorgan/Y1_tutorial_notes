About these notes

These notes accompany the tutorial questions for Y1 Physical Chemistry at the University of Bath. They are intended to help you understand the chemistry behind the calculations—what is physically happening, and why. Throughout these notes, the emphasis is on thinking through each problem before reaching for an equation.

These notes are not a replacement for the tutorial itself, nor for your lecture notes. They are a companion resource, designed to be read alongside the tutorial questions.
